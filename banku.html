Banku

Ingredients

  • Corn dough
  • Cassava dough
  • Salt
  • Water, as needed

Instructions

Step 1

Mix together the corn and cassava dough with water until smooth, then transfer to high heat and stir continiously.

Step 2

Depending on how moist the processed dough was at the start, more water may be added so far as the consistency is solid, but not too firm.

Step 3

Turn the heat down to medium and allow the banku to steam in the pot, surrounded by 1 or 2 tablespoons of water. This should take 5 minutes.

Step 4

Give the banku one final stir then take off the heat.

Step 5

To serve, scoop the desired amount into a wet bowl and swirl around until balls are formed.

Pepper Sauce

Ingredients

  • One Fresh tomato (chopped)
  • One large onion (sliced)
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Hot pepper
  • Salt
  • Natural spices such as garlic, ginger, rosemary

Instructions

Step 1

Blend all the natural spices and the onion.

Step 2

Heat up oil in a frying or saucepan and add in the blended muxture and stir.

Step 3

Let them cook for a minute and add in the chopped tomato and seasoning cubes. Add some salt and pepper to taste.

Step 4

Stir gently, reduce the heat, lid the pan, and allow them to simmer until you get a juicy and spicy sauce.

Your Banku and Pepper Sauce is ready to serve!
