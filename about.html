About

This site is to help both ghanaians and foreigners in preparing some ghanaian foods. It provides the necessary ingredients and steps involved in preparing the various meals listed on the site. We believe this will help people who do not know how to cook some foods as listed on the site to plan and prepare them.

The site is user friendly thus enabling everyone to be able to navigate and find whicherver food it is they want to learn how to prepare
