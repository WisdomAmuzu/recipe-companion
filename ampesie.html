Boiled Yam

Ingredients

  • A tuber of yam
  • Salt
  • Water, as needed

Instructions

Step 1

Peel and Slice the Yam

Step 2

Wash the slices

Step 3

Place in a sauce pan filled with water, make sure the water covers the yam

Step 4

Add a pinch of salt. You can also add little suger for added sweetness

Step 5

Place on medium heat and allow to cook for about 15 to 20 mins

Bonus Step

check with a fork and if the yam easily breaks, the yam is done
